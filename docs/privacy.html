Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Who We Are

HitTheBell Pro is a browser extension operated by GalactoseGalaxy. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at support@galactosegalaxy.com.

What Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate the extension:

  • YouTube channel data — the channels you follow, their latest video information, and whether you have marked a video as watched. This is stored locally in your browser.
  • Extension settings — preferences such as theme and Shorts filter. Stored locally in your browser.
  • Email address — only if you choose to enable cross-device sync or use the account restore feature. Used solely to identify your account and send a one-time sign-in code.
  • Subscription status — whether your subscription is active, stored locally and verified against our server. Billing is handled by Paddle.com; we do not store payment card details.

How We Use Your Data

  • To display your followed channels and their latest uploads
  • To send desktop notifications when a new video is posted
  • To sync your channel list across devices (if you opt in)
  • To verify your subscription status
  • To send a one-time sign-in code when you request account restore

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

  • YouTube Data API — used to fetch channel and video information. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Paddle.com — our payment processor and Merchant of Record. Paddle collects billing information directly. See Paddle's Privacy Policy.
  • Postmark — used to send one-time sign-in codes by email. Only your email address is shared for this purpose.

Data Storage

Channel and settings data is stored locally in your browser using the WebExtensions storage API. If you enable sync, your channel list is also stored on our servers hosted in the United States. By using the sync feature, you consent to your data being transferred to and processed in the United States.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly in accordance with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Your Rights

You can delete your local data at any time by removing the extension or clearing its storage from your browser settings. To request deletion of any data held on our servers, contact support@galactosegalaxy.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the extension after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
