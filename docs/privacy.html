Privacy Policy

Updated: March 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how HitTheBell ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the HitTheBell browser extension and related services (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

  • Account and billing info: Email address and billing metadata provided by our payment processor (e.g., Paddle).
  • Extension settings: Preferences such as theme and Shorts filtering, stored in your browser storage.
  • Channel tracking data: The channel IDs you follow and the latest upload metadata needed to show new uploads.
  • Support communications: Information you provide when you contact us.

2. How We Use Information

We use the data above to:

  • Provide and operate the Service.
  • Sync channel data across your devices.
  • Send transactional emails such as restore codes.
  • Process subscriptions, billing, and access status.
  • Respond to support requests.

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers such as email delivery (Postmark), payment processing (Paddle), and hosting/database providers.
  • Legal and safety requirements if required by law or to protect our users and systems.

4. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as needed to operate the Service or comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account data by contacting support.

5. Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information, including encryption in transit and limited access controls. No system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

6. Your Choices

You can:

  • Uninstall the extension to stop collection of browser data.
  • Contact support to request access or deletion of your data.
  • Manage subscription status through the billing portal.

7. Children

The Service is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required by local law). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. International Transfers

If you access the Service outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries where our providers operate.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are material, we will provide notice as required by law.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at support@galactosegalaxy.com.
