HitTheBell

Updated: March 26, 2026

HitTheBell is a browser extension that allows users to follow YouTube channels and receive desktop notifications on new uploads. The extension's dashboard shows the list of followed channels with their most recent uploaded video. Up to 30 channels can be followed, and YouTube Shorts can be excluded from the video list. The channel list can also be synced across multiple devices.
